10 Best Beach Resorts With Amazing Views in Thailand

  • May 27, 2017
  • ikiw
  • Uncategorized

With numerous beautiful tropical islands, Thailand has a plethora of fine beach resorts. This list presents the 10 beach resorts with amazing view in Thailand. Most of the luxury beach resorts can be found on the islands of Koh Samui, Krabi, Pattaya and Phuket.

The best prices for accommodation can be found during Thailand’s low season, which not surprisingly also coincides with the region’s monsoon season.

Whether you are here for a romantic getaway or family vacation or a special event, Thailand has it all for your dream.

Le Meridien Koh Samui Resort & Spa

This is located just 20 minutes away from Samui International Airport on Lamai Beach. This resort is known for its breathtaking view. If you are searching for a luxurious hideaway just visit this place. Choose from Suite features such as the open-air terrace, enclosed verandah, private plunge pool or direct access to a shared pool. You have 14 luxury villas offered with exclusive privacy and find this place better than ever.

Melati Beach Resort & Spa, Koh Samui

A perfect place for honeymooners, nature enthusiasts and holidaymakers, it is located along the Thongson Bay in Samui Island. Available in six categories, the 77 villas and suites at the resort are designed for a luxurious stay. The room offers you the sight of gleaming white sand and clear blue sea waters. Guests can have a memorable time having dinner on the beach or barbecue. For a hearty Indian meal, visit Curry Pot Indian restaurant, just 10 minutes drive away from here.

Cape Dara Resort

Cape Dara Resort welcomes you to experience five-star stay at a six-star location. Elevated 26 stories above a quiet residential section of North Pattaya, the resort stands in the lush natural surroundings including its own private beach on alluring Pattaya Bay. At Cape Dara, guests enjoy the serenity of a seaside retreat, away from the hectic schedule for leisure.

Royal Cliff Beach Hotel, Pattaya

 This resort is a combination of royal treatment with a great view. Located 800 meters from famed Wat Khao Phra Bat, this place offers a beautiful stay with a 10-minute drive from the teeming Pattaya city center. And please do not forget to grab the breakfast because trust me it’s a huge affair here.

Tubkaak Krabi Boutique Hotel, Krabi

If the wedding is on your cards I suggest planning a stay here. This resort assures a heavenly romantic ambiance with the most beautiful sunsets against limestone cliffs and the fresh sea breeze leaves your skin feeling smooth. Tubkaak literally means ‘home for visitors’, the beach is a hidden corner of the earth facing the stunning archipelago of 13 islands that rise dramatically from the calm Andaman Sea.

Centara Grand Beach Resort & Villas, Krabi

Adding to your basket the world of smiles, this resort is just a 1-minutee walk from the beach located on the private Pai Plong Bay. Imagine a beach on your own. Whether holidaying with the family or with your loved one, Centara Grand Beach Resort & Villas Krabi offers you a corner of paradise.

Read Also: List of Shopping Places in Dubai

Sensimar Koh Samui

 This is an Adults only resort, located along the Mae Nam Beach. Located on the northern coast of Samui, the resort is a 10-minute drive from the popular Friday night walking street and Fisherman’s Village. Samui Airport and the Chaweng Beach area are both a 20-minute drive away. The exotic massage and spa therapy here is highly recommendable.

GLOW Elixir Koh Yao Yai

It is located on 300 meters of shady beach along with their private bay. You will find here individual sea view bungalows, private pools, tropical gardens and what not. This is a must go place to enjoy the tranquility and experience the natural side of Phuket.

Renaissance Phuket Resort & Spa

This property is 1 minute walk from the beach, offering luxurious accommodation on the white sands of Mai Khao Beach. It features a large outdoor pool, jogging and biking trails, and 5 restaurants. With gracious design and luxurious elegance seamlessly blending with a breathtaking island view, Couples particularly love this location

A stay at any of the best beach resorts we have selected is sure to be a perpetual experience of luxury, comfort and relaxation all in your budget. You are sure to enjoy an overwhelming vacation with fresh sea breezes, the gentle lapping of the Sea and breath-taking views.
